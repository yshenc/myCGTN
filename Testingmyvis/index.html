70 years change of data interpretation

Problem: How we discovered and collected this data?

From 1949-2019,《The People's Daily》 fully recorded the important cultural exchange activities and management policy changes of foreigners in China in the 70 years of the New China. The "DSGC Data Reporting International Communication Task Force" of the Communication University of China was obtained from the People's Daily database. From March 1949 to August 2019, a total of 1,940,000 articles were reported. Through a three-stage keyword algorithm, 86,558reporsts on humanistic exchanges of foreigners in China were obtained, and 22,334 foreigners were analyzed through manual identification content analysis. The report on China's management service policy finally compiled 1285 pieces of effective analysis data.
